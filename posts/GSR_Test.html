One of my first joy rides in my 2014 Volkswagen Beetle GSR, number 239 out of the 3500 made worldwide, was a trip to the end of Sanibel-Captiva Island off the coast of Southwest Florida.

This photo was shot on my Canon R50. This little yellow bug has been so much fun. Always an eye-catcher for its strange color-scheme. Was introduced to the vehicle by the video game released the same year, DRIVECLUB by Evolution Studios.
